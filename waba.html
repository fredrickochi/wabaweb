About Us

WabaYetu is a project that is developed a group of young kenyan youths, who have decided to create an impact in the current Global Crisis of globalwarming and climate change. water being one of the main compound that support life. We decided to specialize our solutions water-based.WabaYetu is an Organization that brings together the user(Consumer), the supplier and the specialists in one space where they can interact, thus leading to an organized and efficient market environment between them.The suppliers in this case are the water companies, and the Specialists are the water treatment facilities and analysists.

Our Services

1.Location of water treatment facilities based on your location

2.Free Education on water management skills

3.Free delivery of water upon purchase

4.Water Companies within your locaation

5.Water online shop
